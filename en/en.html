THE ANCHOVIES

A MEDITERRANEAN PRODUCT


The anchovy is a blue fish and an emblematic ingredient of the Mediterranean diet. Olive oil from the best Spanish olive trees also benefits human health.

The consumption of anchovies is highly recommended to maintain a balanced diet, rich in Omega 3 that helps regulate cholesterol levels.

Anchoves de l'Escala S.A. It also offers anchovy fillets in organic extra virgin olive oil.
